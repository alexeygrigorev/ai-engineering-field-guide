The Role
Architect and implement reusable Angular UI frameworks and real-time chat components. Summary Generated by Built In

We’re building a world of health around every individual — shaping a more connected, convenient and compassionate health experience. At CVS Health®, you’ll be surrounded by passionate colleagues who care deeply, innovate with purpose, hold ourselves accountable and prioritize safety and quality in everything we do. Join us and be part of something bigger – helping to simplify health care one person, one family and one community at a time.

Position Summary

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Software Development Engineer with deep expertise in Angular to help shape and scale our next‑generation platform products. This role will focus heavily on building and optimizing real-time chat experiences and supporting a broad range of chat use cases across the enterprise.
The Senior Software Development Engineer will architect and develop reusable UI frameworks, enhance platform-wide capabilities, and collaborate closely with Product, Backend, Architecture, and UX teams to deliver seamless, performant, and reliable chat solutions used across multiple business lines.
Work Breakdown Structure for This Role
Hands-On Engineering – 75%
  • Develop and enhance real-time UI components for platform products and reusable Angular frameworks.
  • Contribute to DevOps activities, including CI/CD pipelines, deployment automation, and environment support.
  • Support cloud integrations, configuration, and troubleshooting within Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for UI and platform product services.
Cross-Functional Collaboration – 25%
  • Partner with Product to refine requirements, provide estimates, and align on roadmap priorities.
  • Work with Architecture to ensure front-end solutions align with enterprise platform standards.
  • Collaborate with Design to implement accessible, user-centered, and pixel-perfect interfaces.
  • Engage with engineering peers through code reviews, mentoring, and cross-team coordination.
Required Qualifications
  • 8+ years of front-end/UI development experience.
  • Expert-level proficiency in Angular (Angular 10+), TypeScript, RxJS, HTML5, and CSS3/SASS.
  • Strong experience designing large-scale Angular component libraries and design systems.
  • Experience leveraging AI-assisted development tools (e.g., GitHub Copilot, code generation, automated test generation).
  • Experience integrating or consuming AI/ML-powered APIs (e.g., NLP services, chat intelligence, recommendation APIs).
  • Hands-on experience building platform products and shared UI frameworks.
  • Deep understanding of modern state management patterns.
  • Experience integrating REST APIs and WebSockets for real-time features.
  • Strong knowledge of browser behavior, performance optimization, and responsive design.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience with GraphQL.
  • Experience leveraging container-based technologies.
  • Experience with unit, integration, and functional testing frameworks.
  • Background working with complex systems and solving highly analytical problems.
  • Experience implementing AI-based features in UI, such as smart suggestions, automated chat insights, or generative UI enhancements.
  • Familiarity with LLM concepts and prompt engineering for building intelligent chat & search experience components.
  • Experience with cloud-hosted AI services (Google Vertex AI, Azure OpenAI, etc.).
Education
  • Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or related field-or equivalent relevant experience

What We Do

CVS Health is the leading health solutions company that delivers care in ways no one else can. And we do it all with heart, each and every day.
