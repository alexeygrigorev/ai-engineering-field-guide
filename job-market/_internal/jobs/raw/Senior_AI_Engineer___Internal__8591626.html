About Securly

Tech Giant Scale. EdTech Innovation. Unmatched Student Impact.

Securly is the market leader in AI-powered student safety and wellness solutions, trusted by more than 20 million students across 20,000+ schools worldwide. Our mission is to help schools create safer, more supportive learning environments for every student.

About the Role

We are seeking a Senior AI Engineer – Internal Tools & Systems to design, build, and operate production-grade, AI-powered internal tools that increase organizational efficiency and reduce manual effort across teams such as Finance, Sales, Customer Success, Support, and Product.

This role is centered on practical, applied AI. You will build systems that are reliable, adopted, and embedded directly into day-to-day workflows—not experiments or proofs of concept. You will own solutions end-to-end, from problem definition through deployment and iteration, working closely with leadership and cross-functional stakeholders.

This is a senior individual contributor role for engineers who enjoy building full-stack internal systems and applying AI responsibly to deliver meaningful business impact.

Location: Pune, Maharashtra, India
Work Type: Hybrid (onsite 2 days per week)
Compensation: ₹48–64 LPA (based on experience and demonstrated capability)

 

What Success Looks Like

- Internal AI tools meaningfully improve how teams work by increasing efficiency and reducing cycle time.
- AI systems are production-ready, reliable, and trusted for real operational use.
- Tools see sustained, repeat usage across multiple business units.
- Clear, repeatable patterns exist for evaluating, building, and deploying internal AI solutions.
- Stakeholders have clarity on priorities, timelines, and tradeoffs.
- Impact is demonstrated through outcomes and adoption—not just features shipped.

What You’ll Accomplish in Your First 12 Months

First 90 Days

- Build a strong understanding of internal workflows, data sources, and operational pain points.
- Review existing internal tools, automation, and prior AI initiatives.
- Deliver early improvements or prototypes such as AI-assisted search, summarization, or workflow support.

First 180 Days

- Design and launch production-ready internal AI tools that reduce manual effort or cycle time.
- Improve reliability, usability, and adoption of AI-enabled systems.
- Establish responsible patterns for building, validating, and deploying AI features.

First 360 Days

- Own multiple internal AI systems used regularly across teams.
- Drive adoption, iteration, and long-term maintenance.
- Contribute to internal standards and best practices for AI-enabled tools.

Core ResponsibilitiesInternal Tools & AI Systems Development

- Design and build internal tools used daily across the organization.
- Develop AI-powered capabilities such as internal search, copilots, summarization, classification, and workflow automation.
- Ship full-stack applications using modern frontend frameworks and cloud-native, serverless backend infrastructure.
- Ensure systems are production-ready, reliable, and easy to operate.

Applied AI & Automation

- Apply LLMs and automation techniques to real operational problems.
- Evaluate tradeoffs between accuracy, cost, latency, and usability.
- Integrate AI systems with internal data sources and third-party platforms.

Platform, Infrastructure & Reliability

- Build scalable APIs and services using AWS and serverless architectures.
- Implement data pipelines and background processing using services such as S3, SQS, or similar.
- Ensure observability, logging, and operational stability.

Collaboration & Ownership

- Partner with Finance, Enablement, Sales, Support, Product, and Engineering teams.
- Own features end-to-end, from design through rollout and iteration.
- Communicate clearly about scope, priorities, tradeoffs, and outcomes.

Core Technology Environment 

- Frontend: TypeScript/JavaScript, Next.js (App Router), JSX/TSX, Tailwind CSS, Radix UI, v0 (Vercel)
- Backend: TypeScript/Node.js, serverless APIs, lightweight frameworks
- Cloud: AWS Lambda, S3, SQS
- Data: PostgreSQL, ORM-based access patterns, vector search
- AI: LLM integration, embeddings, RAG-style internal tools

Required Qualifications

- Experience building and operating production software systems applying AI or automation (typically developed through 5+ years of hands-on engineering experience).
- Strong experience building full-stack systems, including modern React-based frontends and cloud-native backends.
- Proficiency in TypeScript/JavaScript and modern React frameworks (Next.js, JSX/TSX, Tailwind CSS).
- Experience applying LLMs or AI-assisted workflows in practical internal or user-facing systems.
- Experience owning features end-to-end, including deployment and ongoing support.
- Demonstrated judgment around reliability, maintainability, and user adoption.
- Experience working effectively across time zones and with cross-functional stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience building internal tools for non-engineering teams.
- Familiarity with AWS serverless services (Lambda, S3, SQS).
- Exposure to vector search, embeddings, RAG-style systems, or AI-enhanced search.
- Experience integrating with platforms such as Salesforce, ticketing, or analytics tools.
- Prior experience operating at a Senior or equivalent IC level.

What We Do

Securly is the market leader in AI-powered student safety and wellness solutions, trusted by over 20 million students across 20,000+ schools worldwide. We believe every child deserves a learning environment that is safe, supportive, and free from harm or distraction.

Since launching the world’s first cloud-based web filter for K–12 in 2013, Securly has been on a mission to redefine what technology can do for student well-being. Today, our award-winning platform uses AI and human insight to keep students safe online, identify signs of bullying, self-harm, or violence, and empower schools to take proactive, compassionate action.

From web filtering and classroom management to mental health support and 24/7 human-verified alerts, our solutions protect, guide, and uplift students every day. Our technology has been credited with preventing more than 2,000 potential tragedies—proof of our commitment to making a life-changing impact at scale.

Operating with the innovation and agility of a startup and the reach of a tech giant, Securly continues to lead the EdTech industry in shaping the future of student wellness, safety, and engagement. We’re proud to be recognized as a Top Place to Work, an EdTech Top 40 company, and one of the most widely adopted K–12 platforms in the U.S.

At Securly, we don’t just build products—we build trust. We partner with educators, counselors, and administrators nationwide to create digital and physical spaces where students can learn, grow, and thrive.
