Digital & AI/ML Engineer

The Role
The Digital & AI/ML Engineer will implement AI pipelines, develop AI-powered applications, and collaborate on AI solutions to improve productivity.
Summary Generated by Built In

Fiduciary Trust International is a premier investment and wealth management firm with a commitment to growing and protecting wealth across generations. We offer a dynamic and collaborative approach to managing wealth for high-net-worth and ultra high-net-worth individuals and families, family offices, endowments, foundations, and institutions. Our investment managers, tax and estate planning professionals work together to develop holistic strategies to optimize clients’ portfolios while mitigating the impact of taxes on their wealth. As a fiduciary, the guidance we provide is in the best interests of our clients, without conflict or competing benefits. We offer boutique customization and deep expertise in specialized investment, tax and planning strategies alongside sophisticated technology and custody platforms.

Fiduciary Trust International is owned by Franklin Templeton, a dynamic firm that spans asset management, wealth management, and fintech, giving us many ways to help investors make progress toward their goals. With clients in over 150 countries and offices on six continents, you’ll get exposed to different cultures, people, and business development happening around the world.

About the Department:

Fiduciary Trust International (FTI), a Franklin Templeton company, is a leading provider of comprehensive wealth management solutions for high-net-worth individuals, families, foundations, and endowments. With a strong fiduciary heritage, FTI offers personalized trust and estate services, investment management, and administrative solutions designed to preserve and grow client wealth across generations. Known for its client-first culture, rigorous risk management, and collaborative approach, Fiduciary Trust International combines deep expertise with the global resources of Franklin Templeton to deliver tailored, long-term financial outcomes.

The AI, Data, and Process Optimization team builds solutions that combine data engineering, analytics, and generative AI to deliver measurable business impact. We work in a collaborative, fast-paced environment, partnering with internal teams to improve productivity and decision-making. Joining this team offers exposure to cutting-edge AI technologies and the opportunity to grow your skills in a startup-like setting within a global enterprise.

How You Will Add Value?

  • You will implement model training, evaluation, and inference pipelines under senior guidance.

  • You will build proofs of concept and internal AI tools to validate ideas quickly.

  • You will engineer features and preprocess data from curated datasets for experiments.

  • You will develop and maintain AI-powered applications like summarization and classification.

  • You will monitor and troubleshoot AI services in production for continuous improvement.

  • You will collaborate with teams to understand pain points and tailor AI solutions.

  • You will maintain clear documentation for models, APIs, and experiments.

  • You will take ownership of smaller projects and escalate risks early.

What Will Help You Be Successful?

Experience, Education & Certifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Machine Learning, or related field (Master’s preferred).

  • 2–4 years in AI/ML engineering or related software engineering roles.

Technical Skills:

  • Strong understanding of machine learning fundamentals and modern deep learning frameworks.

  • Proficiency in Python, SQL and common ML libraries; knowledge of software engineering best practices.

  • Hands-on experience with data preprocessing, model training, and optimization.

  • Familiarity with cloud platforms and AI/ML deployment tools is a plus.

Soft Skills:

  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.

Work Shift Timings - 2:00 PM - 11:00 PM IST
