Hello, We’re Built In

What We’re Up To 

Built In is the only recruitment and employer reputation platform that helps companies measure and shape their reputation in AI search — so they can be visible, trusted, and chosen by top talent.

The HR tech space is filled with AI noise, but the real disruption is happening on the candidate side. By 2028, more candidates will use AI assistants like ChatGPT and Perplexity to discover and apply for jobs — fundamentally changing how talent and employers connect. Built In is the first to build for this new reality, giving companies the ability to understand and influence how they appear in AI-driven search.

Our platform already reaches millions of tech professionals each month, and our 1,800+ customers — from startups to Fortune 100 companies — rely on us to tell authentic stories, showcase opportunities, and attract top talent. With our newest AI-powered tools, we’re evolving Built In into a data-rich, reputation-driven platform that’s shaping the future of hiring

What You’ll Be a Part Of

Built In is 14 years strong but operates with the energy and pace of a startup. We ship fast, learn constantly, and build with purpose. As an engineer here, you’ll have the chance to design and deliver products at the intersection of AI, reputation, and search — directly influencing how millions of professionals and thousands of companies connect.

You’ll join a team that values curiosity, accountability, and being good humans first. Our engineering culture is rooted in collaboration, trust, and continuous improvement.

If you want to build products that matter, shape how AI transforms the future of work, and make a visible impact inside a company that’s redefining its category — this is the opportunity for you

We’re looking for an Applied AI Engineer

We're seeking an experienced Applied AI Engineer to design and develop intelligent, agentic systems that take action on behalf of users. You’ll work at the intersection of AI automation and user experience—building conversational UIs and AI-driven workflows that solve real-world problems with minimal user input.

This is an ideal role for someone who’s passionate about Large Language Models (LLMs), intelligent agents, and the future of user interaction. You’ll have the opportunity to shape how AI behaves autonomously within product ecosystems.

How you’ll contribute
  • Architect and build agentic workflows that can interpret intent, plan tasks, and execute operations on behalf of users.
  • Develop and iterate on chat-based user interfaces for rich, dynamic interactions.
  • Leverage LLM platforms (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) and orchestration tools like LangChain, Semantic Kernel, or custom frameworks.
  • Work cross-functionally with backend engineers, product managers, and designers to integrate conversational features into the platform.
  • Design robust systems for evaluating agent performance, user feedback loops, and improving reliability.
  • Stay current with cutting-edge developments in LLM tools, RAG pipelines, memory/state handling, and prompt engineering.
What you need 
  • 4+ years in a Data Science, Machine Learning, or Applied AI role
  • Deep experience with Python, LLM APIs, and libraries like transformers, langchain, OpenAI, etc.
  • Experience building chatbots or conversational UIs, and strong understanding of natural language processing
  • Solid grasp of SQL, APIs, and structured/semi-structured data
  • Familiarity with vector databases, tool use orchestration, and agent memory design
  • Excellent collaboration and communication skills, especially in cross-functional teams
  • Bonus: experience in reinforcement learning, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), or AI monitoring systems
Why Work With Us
  • Help shape intelligent systems that act autonomously in real-world environments
  • Be part of a product-led, engineering-driven culture
  • Remote-first team with collaborative, flexible work structure
  • Opportunities for leadership and technical ownership in AI development

What We Do

Built In is the AI-powered career platform that connects innovative companies with professionals navigating their careers in a world reshaped by AI.
