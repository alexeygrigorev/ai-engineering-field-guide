Test Automation Engineer-(AI Tools, Python)

The Role
Develop and maintain automation frameworks using Python and pytest, create automated test scripts with Selenium, and integrate tests into Jenkins CI/CD pipelines.
Summary Generated by Built In
Cambium Networks delivers wireless communications that work for businesses, communities, and cities worldwide. Millions of our radios are deployed to connect people, places and things with a unified wireless fabric that spans multiple standards and frequencies of fixed wireless and Wi-Fi, all managed centrally via the cloud. Our multi-gigabit wireless fabric offers a compelling value proposition over traditional fiber and alternative wireless solutions. We work with our Cambium certified ConnectedPartners to deliver purpose-built networks for service provider, enterprise, industrial, and government connectivity solutions in urban, suburban, and rural environments, with wireless that just works.

Check out our career opportunities at www.cambiumnetworks.com/careers

Job Summary:
We are seeking a Senior Automation Engineer with 5+ years of experience to drive our test automation initiatives. This role requires strong technical expertise in Python-based automation and mandatory proficiency in using AI tools to enhance productivity and code quality.

Key Responsibilities:
•Design and maintain enterprise-grade automation frameworks using Python and pytest
•Develop automated test suites for web applications (Selenium) and RESTful APIs
•Own CI/CD pipeline integration and optimization using Jenkins
•Lead code reviews, establish automation standards, and mentor team members
•Actively use AI coding assistants (GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) for development, debugging, and problem-solving
•Drive AI tool adoption within the team and contribute to building an AI-first engineering culture

Required Skills:
•5+ years of hands-on test automation experience
•Expert-level Python programming skills
•Strong experience with pytest, Selenium, and RESTful API testing
•Solid understanding of CI/CD practices and Jenkins
•Proficiency with Git and version control workflows
•Proven hands-on experience using AI coding assistants in daily development work (mandatory)
•Strong leadership, mentoring, and communication skills

Nice to Have:
•Experience with NMS (Network Management Systems) automation
•Docker/containerization experience
•Knowledge of AI/ML concepts in testing
•Cloud platform exposure (AWS, Azure, GCP)
