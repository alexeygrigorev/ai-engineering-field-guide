Summary

The AI Engineer on the AI Product & Engineering team is responsible for crafting, building, and overseeing AI-powered solutions tailored for the OneStream platform. This position involves continuously converting business problems into polished, commercially viable applications leveraging AI and computer science techniques. This position requires a robust grasp of modern software design principles, including frontend design and web frameworks, alongside proficiency in UX/UI design methodology and best practices. Collaboration with diverse engineering and services teams is essential to deliver comprehensive product support, ensure product cycle maintenance, and drive continuous product improvements. The AI Product and Engineering team is focused on bridging the gap between AI research and AI products, bringing tangible and quantifiable value to our end users. Our AI solutions incorporate AI and machine learning spectrum, including (but not limited to) time series forecasting, anomaly detection, and natural language processing. Our mission is to lower the barrier for organizations and people to get value out of AI capabilities.


Primary Duties and Responsibilities

  • Build and enhance user interfaces using both native OneStream capabilities and modern web frameworks.
  • Write and upkeep code that is efficient, reusable, dependable, and abides by modern software design principles.
  • Own the end-to-end UI/UX process: from wireframing and prototyping to implementation.
  • Guarantee peak performance, rendering, and responsiveness of AI Services solutions.
  • Identify and address bottlenecks and bugs, and devise solutions to mitigate or eliminate these issues.
  • Help to preserve code quality, organization, and automation.
  • Write and define software bugs, enhancements, and story tickets in a software issue-tracking system.
  • Collaborate with Product Specialists and Quality Assurance Testers to effectively.
  • Write documentation in the form of how-to guides to get other stakeholders familiar with the products you develop.

Required Education and Experience

  • Bachelors Degree or similar work experience.
  • CS/DS/EE/CE Degree or similar experience.
  • 1-2 years of experience with object-oriented programming.
  • 1-2 years of strong programming experience with one of the following: C++, C#, Java.
  • Experience with relational databases.

Preferred Education and Experience

  • Experience with modern web development frameworks (React preferred).
  • Experience with design tools such as Figma (or similar) for creating wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity UI mockups.
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Azure and related technologies.
  • Familiarity with AI systems, algorithms, and subdomains (time-series, classifications, clustering, anomaly, etc.).
  • Experience with Consolidation Software, such as: OneStream, HFM, SAP BOFC, SAP, IBM Cognos, etc.
  • Recent focus on Microsoft technologies.

  • Self-Starter to decompose a problem and plan a solution.

  • Excellent written and verbal interpersonal skills.

What We Do

OneStream Software provides a market-leading intelligent finance platform that reduces the complexity of financial operations. OneStream™ unleashes the power of finance by unifying corporate performance management (CPM) processes such as planning, financial close & consolidation, reporting, and analytics through a single, extensible solution. We empower the enterprise with financial and operational insights to support faster and more informed decision-making. All in a cloud platform designed to continually evolve and scale with your organization.

OneStream is an independent software company backed by private equity investors KKR, D1 Capital Partners, Tiger Global, and IGSB. Our primary mission is to deliver 100% customer success, which we’ve done successfully since our inception. To learn more visit www.onestreamsoftware.com.
