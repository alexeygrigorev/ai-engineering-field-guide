WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE IN THIS POSITION: As an nVent AI/ML Engineer, you will design, develop, and deploy production AI/ML solutions that deliver measurable business value across our $4B global organization. Working within our Data & Analytics organization, you'll leverage our enterprise Snowflake data platform and AWS infrastructure to build models that address real business challenges. The ideal candidate combines strong ML engineering skills with the ability to translate business problems into technical solutions, deploy production-grade systems, and work effectively with business stakeholders who may have limited AI expertise. Key Responsibilities
• Solution Development
o Partner with business stakeholders to understand challenges and translate those challenges into AI/ML solution delivery
o Design and implement ML models and AI solutions that address specific business problems with measurable ROI (cost reduction, revenue growth, efficiency gains)
o Conduct feasibility assessments for proposed AI use cases, evaluating data availability, technical approach, and expected business value
o Present findings and recommendations to business leaders and technical audiences, adjusting communication style for varying levels of AI literacy
• Model Development & Deployment
o Build, train, validate, and deploy machine learning models using Snowflake ML capabilities and AWS services (SageMaker, Bedrock, Lambda)

o Create computer vision applications for quality inspection, safety monitoring, and document understanding
o Establish MLOps practices including model versioning, monitoring, retraining pipelines, and performance tracking
o Implement human-in-the-loop workflows for high-stakes decisions requiring human oversight

What We Do

nVent is a leading global provider of electrical connection and protection solutions. We believe our inventive electrical solutions enable safer systems and ensure a more secure world. We design, manufacture, market, install and service high performance products and solutions that connect and protect some of the world's most sensitive equipment, buildings and critical processes. We offer a comprehensive range of systems protection and electrical connections solutions across industry-leading brands that are recognized globally for quality, reliability and innovation.

Our principal office is in London and our management office in the United States is in Minneapolis. Our robust portfolio of leading electrical product brands dates back more than 100 years and includes nVent CADDY, ERICO, HOFFMAN, ILSCO, SCHROFF and TRACHTE. Learn more at www.nvent.com.
