Software Engineer, AI/ML

Automation and agents for modern finance teams.
The Role
Join the AI team to build automation features in AI/ML, owning projects from concept to integration, and collaborating with leadership.
Summary Generated by Built In

Tabs is the leading AI-native revenue platform for modern finance and accounting teams. Tabs agents automates the entire contract-to-cash lifecycle, including billing, collections, revenue recognition, and reporting, to help teams eliminate manual work and accelerate cash flow.

High-growth companies like Cursor and Statsig rely on Tabs to generate invoices directly from contracts, reconcile payments in real time, and automate ASC 606 compliance.

Founded in 2023, Tabs has raised over $91 million from Lightspeed Venture Partners, General Catalyst, and Primary. The team is headquartered in New York and brings deep expertise in finance and AI.

What We Do

We are enabling the one person finance team…

Artificial intelligence products that automate operational management of accounting, communications, and reporting. Finance leaders shouldn’t worry about updating new points of contacts for an invoice; our systems enable them to deliver strategy and vision to their business.

The Job

We are putting together a tight team of innovative product engineers to join the AI team full stack. This is an in person role in New York City.

  • You will own building automation, AI, and ML features from concept, release, and integration into the product.

  • We are looking beyond pattern matching, this isn’t a solved problem and we are looking for engineers who will generate new insights and solutions.

  • You will work closely with company leadership and the founding team to deliver high impact projects with direct impact on market positioning of the business.

Who You Are

  • Proven ability to build green field products, get it 80% right, and learn from the missing 20%.

  • Experience taking ownership of projects and driving them through to completion

  • 3-5 years of full stack experience in engineering that directly impacts the delivery of product and features to users

  • 1+ year of experience on products that deliver AI/ML inference to end users. Can be anywhere in that lifecycle, from training machine learning systems, to creating the interfaces users use to navigate inference results.

  • An interest and passion for AI/ML systems, if you haven’t had the opportunity at work to build AI/ML systems but have personal projects in the space we would love to hear about them.

  • We don’t care what stack you’ve built in the past, but you will be building with Python, Typescript, and NextJs (bonus points if this is already your stack)

Exceptions to the above requirements are made for candidates with exceptional stories. If this is you, we will consider you regardless of the guidelines above.

Our Phenotype

We are not looking for people who like to coast or shop offers for value. We are looking for gunners who are attracted to non-traditional success. A specific type of person does well here:

  • You pride yourself on grit and creativity though you know you may not be the smartest in the room.

  • You are obsessed with building and have a clear track record of ownership and shipping highly creative, innovative works.

  • You are energized by working in-person with colleagues four or more days a week.

  • You are opinionated on how product should be built and know when to fight for your ideas (and when to dissent and commit)

  • When you see someone spilled coffee, you grab a mop.

We’re a NYC-based team working together in our Manhattan office.

Even if you don’t meet 100% of the qualifications, we encourage you to apply. We care most about curiosity, craft, and drive.

Perks and Benefits (Full-time Employees)

  • Competitive compensation and equity

  • Unlimited PTO

  • Up to 100% employer covered monthly healthcare premium (medical, dental, vision)

  • Lunch provided via Sharebite, plus dinner for any later office days.

  • Parental leave up to 12 weeks

  • Tax free commuter and parking benefits

  • Voluntary insurances (Life, Hospital, Critical Illness, Accident)

  • Employee Assistance Program (Rightway)

  • 401k

What We Do

Tabs is a revenue automation platform that helps B2B businesses automate invoice-to-cash workflows like billing, receivables, payments, rev rec, and reporting.

Finance software hasn’t kept pace. For decades, companies have relied on outdated systems that bury teams in manual work. We started Tabs to fix that, building the modern revenue stack: AI-native automation that turns contracts into action, eliminates errors, and powers finance with speed and accuracy.

Why Work With Us

Join us on the journey. Founded by operators who’ve spent decades scaling companies and backed by Lightspeed, General Catalyst, and Primary, Tabs is reimagining how revenue runs from contract to cash.

We’re a fast-moving, thoughtful team built around ownership, impact, and solving problems that matter—and we’re just getting started.
